Combinations of three things

Our allCombs function is more general but it still only lets us generate combinations of two things. It is probably not not immediately obvious how to extend your allCombs function to do that. First implement an allCombs3 function that generates combinations of 3 things. Donâ€™t try to do anything fancy yet. Just use the most straightforward approach you can think of.
